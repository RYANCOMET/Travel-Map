Rail route generator

Builds and improves rail-routes.json. Fast UI version with OSM segment fallback and chunked OSM fallback for dense routes. It can generate missing routes, check saved route quality, and retry routes that failed or look too coarse. This version requires at least 4 points per kilometre.
